Origine

Origin is a Japanese anime film directed by Keiichi Sugiyama and released in 2006.
95 minutes long, Origin is a story that takes place 300 years after our era, on an Earth now controlled by the spirits of the forest who take revenge on Men after they destroyed the planet.

This film is compared to the style of Hayao Miyazaki, director and co-founder of the famous Studio Ghibli, who is also used to conveying messages related to ecology in his achievements.

A world divided in two

Ragna, which is the civilization that wishes to return to the old civilization, waging war against the spirits of nature.

The Neutral City, which wishes to live in harmony with the spirits of the forest.

Hard awakening

One day, Agito, the main character, accidentally wakes up a young girl named Toola.
The latter will quickly realize that Toola is a young girl from the past who had remained asleep for nearly 300 years.

Resident of the Neutral City, Agito will then bring this young girl to the authorities who will wish to give the young woman as an offering to nature, the spirits of the forest asking for the young girl, claiming that she will create big problems in the world. if it is not offered to them.

The existence of Toola will come to the ears of the authorities of Ragna who will want the young woman after learning that she was the daughter of a famous scientist.

This will rekindle the tensions between the two civilizations who will both want to appropriate the girl.

It's a film that I really like because in addition to the graphic style and its music, the message of the film is a message that I find very beautiful.

It is a film that makes us reflect on our view of our relationship to nature and to others.
